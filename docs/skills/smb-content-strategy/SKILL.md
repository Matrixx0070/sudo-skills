## When to use

Use this when you need a data-backed plan for what to promote next month, not finished posts. It reads your sales history and turns it into a prioritized brief that a creator (or smb-canva-creator) can execute later.

## METHOD

1. Pull sales data for the trailing 3-12 months: revenue, units, and margin by product or service.
2. Identify top performers worth amplifying and slow movers that may need a push, a bundle, or retirement. Note margin, not just volume.
3. Detect seasonality — recurring peaks and troughs — and flag what typically sells in the coming 30 days.
4. Map findings to content themes and a rough cadence, prioritizing high-margin winners and timely seasonal plays.
5. Present the brief for the owner's approval. This skill plans only; it does not create designs, schedule posts, or spend on ads.

## OUTPUT FORMAT

A short findings summary (top performers, slow movers, seasonal notes) with the numbers cited. Then a prioritized 30-day content brief: theme, rationale tied to the data, target product, suggested formats, and cadence. End with "Approve this brief to move into production."
