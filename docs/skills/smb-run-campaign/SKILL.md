When to use: Run this when the owner wants to launch a promotion, seasonal push, or re-engagement campaign and wants it built end to end rather than piece by piece. Each stage is gated: the owner approves before the next begins.

METHOD
1. Analyze sales: pull top and bottom sellers, recent trends, and customer segments. Recommend a campaign angle and target audience. Owner approves the angle.
2. Content brief: define the offer, message, channel(s), timing, and success metric. Owner approves the brief.
3. Assets: draft the copy and any subject lines, captions, or image prompts for the approved channels. Owner reviews and edits.
4. Audience: pull the recipient segment from the connected CRM and show the count and filters. Owner confirms the list.
5. Send: schedule or send through the CRM only after final owner approval. Nothing reaches a customer before that click.
6. Report back the send confirmation and where to watch results.

OUTPUT FORMAT
- Stage-by-stage output, each ending with an explicit "Approve to continue?" gate.
- Sales analysis summary, then brief, then assets, then audience count, then send confirmation.
- Any step that contacts customers or spends money waits for owner approval.
