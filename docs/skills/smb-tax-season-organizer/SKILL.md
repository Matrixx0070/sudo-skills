When to use: Run this ahead of a quarterly estimated-tax deadline or at year-end 1099 time, when the owner needs their numbers and documents gathered into something an accountant can use. This organizes; it does not file or give tax advice.

METHOD
1. Confirm the mode: quarterly estimate or year-end 1099 packet, and the tax period.
2. Pull income and expense totals from connected accounting or bank data, categorized by common tax buckets. Flag gaps and uncategorized items for the owner to resolve.
3. For quarterly mode: summarize period income, deductible expenses, and a rough estimate range, clearly labeled as an estimate to confirm with the accountant.
4. For 1099 mode: list contractors paid over the reporting threshold, checking for missing W-9 details (name, TIN, address) and flagging incomplete records.
5. Assemble the handoff packet: organized summary, supporting document checklist, and open questions.
6. Deliver to the owner to review before it goes to the accountant. The owner approves any external send.

OUTPUT FORMAT
- Mode and period header.
- Categorized income/expense summary (or contractor table for 1099).
- "Missing or needs your attention" list.
- A handoff packet outline plus a document checklist.
- Disclaimer: figures are for accountant review, not filed advice.
